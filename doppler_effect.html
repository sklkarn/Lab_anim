The Doppler Effect occurs when a source of sound and an observer move relative to each other. Waves compress (higher pitch) when approaching and stretch (lower pitch) when receding. At Mach 1 a sonic boom occurs. Formula: f' = f₀ · (v ± v_obs) / (v ∓ v_src)
