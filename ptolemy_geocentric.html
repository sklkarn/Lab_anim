The Almagest — Earth at the Centre of All Heavens

Tempo
The Deferent
Each planet moves on a great circle called the deferent, centred not on Earth but on a small offset point — the eccentric.
The Epicycle
Upon the deferent rides a smaller circle, the epicycle. The planet itself moves upon this inner wheel, producing looping retrograde motion.
The Equant
Uniform angular speed is measured not from Earth but from a third point, the equant — Ptolemy's boldest mathematical device.
The Primum Mobile
Beyond all planets lies the outermost sphere of fixed stars, the Primum Mobile, which carries all the heavens in its daily rotation.
