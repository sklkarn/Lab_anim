Types of Equilibrium

Click any canvas to nudge the ball — observe how each surface responds to a small displacement

STABLE EQUILIBRIUM Bowl / Funnel
Ball rests at the bottom of the bowl.
Displaced → restoring force returns it → oscillates → settles back.
PE is minimum at equilibrium.
UNSTABLE EQUILIBRIUM Inverted / Hill
Ball balanced at the top of the hill.
Any displacement → diverging force → ball rolls away permanently.
PE is maximum at equilibrium.
NEUTRAL EQUILIBRIUM Flat Surface
Ball rests on a flat surface.
Displaced → no restoring or diverging force → stays wherever placed.
PE is constant everywhere.
