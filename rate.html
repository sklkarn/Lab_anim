Quiz 1: Related Rates Problems

1. Air is being pumped into a spherical balloon at a rate of 5 cm³/min. How fast is the radius increasing when the diameter is 20 cm?

2. A 15-ft ladder leans against a wall. The bottom slides away from the wall at 1/4 ft/sec. How fast is the top of the ladder sliding down the wall when the bottom is 9 ft from the wall?

3. The radius of a circle is increasing at 0.4 m/s. At what rate is the area increasing when the circumference is 60π m?

4. Water is leaking from an inverted conical tank at 3 m³/min. The tank has height 4 m and base radius 2 m. How fast is the water level dropping when the water height is 2 m?

Quiz 2: Instantaneous Rates & Differentials

1. The position of a particle is s(t) = 2t³ - 12t² + 8. Find the instantaneous velocity at t = 3.

2. Approximate the change in volume of a sphere when the radius changes from 5 cm to 5.1 cm (use differentials).

3. A cube’s edge increases at 2 cm/min. How fast is the surface area increasing when the edge is 15 cm?
