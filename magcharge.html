Magnetic Force on a Moving Charge

F = qv × B  ·  Lorentz Force Law  ·  Right-Hand Rule

Attraction — F toward wire
Current I ↑   Charge v ↑ (same as I)
B field circles CCW  ·  F = qv×B → toward wire
Repulsion — F away from wire
Current I ↑   Charge v ↓ (opposite I)
B field circles CCW  ·  F = qv×B → away from wire

B = μ₀I / 2πr  (circumferential, right-hand rule)
