Three effects change g with latitude:
1. Shape — Earth is oblate (flattened at poles), poles are ~21 km closer to centre → stronger g.
2. Rotation — centrifugal effect is max at equator, zero at poles → reduces g at equator.
3. Combined — Somigliana's international gravity formula accounts for both.
