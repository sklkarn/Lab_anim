Laws of Reflection
FIRST LAW The angle of incidence equals the angle of reflection, both measured from the normal at the point of incidence.
SECOND LAW The incident ray, reflected ray, and normal all lie in the same plane. The image is as far behind the mirror as the object is in front.
THIRD LAW The image formed is virtual, erect, and laterally inverted, of the same size as the object.
