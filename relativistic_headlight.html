Relativistic Aberration & Headlight Beaming (Source Frame → Lab Frame)
Relativistic Doppler Effect: Unlike sound, light Doppler shift is purely relativistic — there is no medium. Approaching sources blueshift (higher freq), receding sources redshift (lower freq). The Headlight Effect: at relativistic speeds, a source emitting light isotropically in its rest frame beams almost all photons forward in the lab frame — like a lighthouse turned into a laser.
