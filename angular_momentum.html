L = I · ω  ·  I = Σ mᵢrᵢ²  ·  When I ↓ → ω ↑ (L stays constant)
L = I·ω = constant — In the absence of external torque, angular momentum is conserved. The moment of inertia I depends on mass distribution. Pull arms in → I decreases → ω increases proportionally. This is why a spinning skater speeds up when pulling arms inward.
