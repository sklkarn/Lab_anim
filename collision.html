Elastic — objects bounce: v₁' = ((m₁−m₂)v₁ + 2m₂v₂)/(m₁+m₂)  ·  v₂' = ((m₂−m₁)v₂ + 2m₁v₁)/(m₁+m₂)
Inelastic — objects stick together and travel as one: v' = (m₁v₁ + m₂v₂)/(m₁+m₂)  ·  kinetic energy is lost to heat & deformation
